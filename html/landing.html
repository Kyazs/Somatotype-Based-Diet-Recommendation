How It Works

Our advanced AI system analyzes your body type in just a few simple steps

1. Input Information

Provide your details, including gender, name, activity level, age, weight, height, and any allergies, so we can create a personalized diet plan tailored to your unique needs.

2. Image Capture

Using computer vision, the system will guide you to pose and capture both a front and side photo of yourself.

3. Measurement Extraction

Our deep learning model analyzes the captured images to accurately extract your body measurements.

4. Somatotype Classification

Using the extracted measurements, the system employs a deterministic algorithm to identify your somatotype.

5. Diet Recommendations

Based on your somatotype, the system will recommend a diet plan tailored to your needs, verified by experts.
